2. Movement Restrictions

Navigation is key. The grid is 15×15, and you must follow these two laws:

· 

Cardinal Movement (N, S, E, W): You can move in a straight line up to your current Rank’s range. (e.g., a Rank C hunter can move 3 tiles up).

· 

· 

Diagonal Movement: Regardless of rank, diagonal steps are strictly limited to 1 tile. You cannot "snipe" someone diagonally from across the map.

· 

3. The Gate System

Gates are your primary source of MP. They spawn in waves every 3 whole rounds (meaning after every living player has finished their turn).

· 

Difficulty Curve: * Early Game: Only Rank E and D gates spawn.

· 

· 

Mid Game: Rank C and B gates start appearing.

· 

· 

Late Game: Rank A gates descend.

· 

· 

Farming: To defeat a gate, you must land on its tile. If your MP is higher than the gate’s MP, you absorb its power. If you are weaker, you will fall.

· 

4. Combat & Runes (Power-ups)

When you land on another player or a Gate, a 5-second Battle Phase begins.

Combat Rules

· 

The hunter with the higher MP wins.

· 

 

The winner absorbs the loser's MP.

· 

· 

In PvP, the loser is eliminated.

· 

The Runes

Runes can only be obtained randomly by defeating Gates. You can only activate them during the 5-second combat window.

· 

⚔️ DOUBLE DAMAGE: Doubles your MP for that battle only.

· 

· 

�� GHOST WALK: Cancels the battle and teleports you to a safe, random location.

· 

· 

�� NETHER SWAP: Swaps your position with a random player. You absorb the MP of whoever loses the resulting proxy battle and then warp to a random living player.

· 

· 

�� RULER'S AUTHORITY: An absolute win against any player or standard Gate (does not work on the Silver Monarch).

· 

5. The Silver Monarch & Winning

The ultimate goal is to defeat the Silver Monarch.

· 

Spawn Condition: The Silver Monarch descends only when one player remains (Total, including AIs).

· 

· 

The Trial: You have 5 turns to reach and defeat the Monarch.

· 

· 

Power Level: The Monarch’s MP ranges from 1,500 to infinity.

· 

· 

Victory: Defeating the Monarch ends the game and grants you Hunter Points (HuP).

· 

· 

The Reawakening: If the last player loses or the 5-turn timer hits zero, everyone respawns, all gates are cleared, and the game continues with Rank S and A gates everywhere.

·
